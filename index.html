01 — How it works

Three steps. Then you’re back to it.

  1. 01

    Order in the app

    Choose your drink and pay in seconds. The app generates a QR code, ready before you reach the tap.

  2. 02

    Scan & place your cup

    Scan the code at the machine and set a cup beneath the tap. No buttons to guess at, no queue to join.

  3. 03

    Watch it pour

    Coffee, matcha or a protein shake, hot or cold, filled in seconds. Take it and get back to whatever matters.
