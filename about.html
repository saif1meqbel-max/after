About us

Made for the hours
when everything else is closed.

After began with a simple, familiar feeling — sitting somewhere late, still working, wanting a proper coffee or matcha, and finding nothing open but a tired vending machine.

So we built the alternative. A calm, self-service drinks system that pours real coffee, real matcha and protein shakes — hot or cold — straight from six taps. You order in the app, scan a QR code at the machine, place your cup, and it fills in seconds. No staff. No waiting. No compromise on the cup.

Designed to sit beautifully in a workplace, a campus, a library or a late-opening space, After is there at 9am and still there at 2am — quietly doing one thing exceptionally well.

The ritual

Order, scan, pour. A few unhurried seconds from tap to cup.

The craft

A concentrate system built for consistency — the same clean cup, every time.

The place

One small, refined footprint. It runs itself, day and night.
